Lab Measurements

Lab measurements are slightly different from the lab goals. Where the lab goals define what the the purpose and definition of the lab is, the measurements are your definition as to what it means for the lab to have been successful.

Often times defining a measurement of success that can actually be measured is difficult. Do your best to determine outcomes and, preferably, metrics that will define success.

An example may be: replace at least 10 automated emails with OpenNMS events and notifications
