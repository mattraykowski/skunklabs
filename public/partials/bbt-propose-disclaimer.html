Are you sure you want to propose a lab?

Creating a new lab proposal is a big deal, make sure you're ready to lead this lab. Here are some helpful guidelines on whether you should propose a lab and good guidelines on successful lab proposals and completed labs.

  • Labs should be small in scale and achievable.
  • Labs should not require a budget of any sort.
  • Labs should be able to be accomplished with a small (1-4 person) team.
  • Labs should have some direct benefit to the organization, such as solving a team problem or creating a new app for patients and families.

Note! Skunk Labs labs are personal-time only labs. If you intend to work on this lab during your regular work hours you must confirm with your manager or supervisor that this is okay and set realistic expectations.
